Research Interests

I am a Cybercrime Researcher from Dutch Police East-Netherlands. My research focuses on cybercrime, economics, mathematical modeling, darknet forums, and ransomware. I explore criminal decision-making, intervention strategies, and economic frameworks to understand digital crime patterns.
